1. **Pattern Recognition > Memorization**: Focus on understanding the underlying pattern
2. **Spaced Repetition**: Revisit problems after 3, 7, and 30 days
3. **Active Recall**: Try to solve without looking at solutions
4. **Time Boxing**: Stick to time limits (E:15, M:30, H:45)
5. **Health Matters**: Take breaks, sleep well, exercise
